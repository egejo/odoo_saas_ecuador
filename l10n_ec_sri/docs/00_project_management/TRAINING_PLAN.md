# TRAINING PLAN
## Ecuador Odoo 18.0 Localization

**Document ID**: SOMA-TRAIN-001
**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: 2026-01-22

---

## 1. TRAINING OBJECTIVES

By the end of training, users will be able to:
1. Create and authorize electronic invoices
2. Generate withholding documents
3. Process payroll with IESS calculations
4. Generate Guías de Remisión
5. Produce ATS reports

---

## 2. TRAINING SCHEDULE

| Session | Topic | Duration | Audience |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| 1 | Odoo Overview + Navigation | 2 hours | All Users |
| 2 | Electronic Invoicing | 3 hours | Accounting |
| 3 | Withholding Documents | 2 hours | Accounting |
| 4 | Purchasing & Liquidaciones | 2 hours | Procurement |
| 5 | Guías de Remisión | 2 hours | Warehouse |
| 6 | POS Electronic Invoicing | 2 hours | Cashiers |
| 7 | Payroll Processing | 3 hours | HR |
| 8 | ATS & Reports | 2 hours | Accounting |

## 3. TRAINING BY ROLE

### 3.1 Accountant
- Electronic Invoice creation
- Withholding processing
- ATS generation
- Bank reconciliation

### 3.2 HR Manager
- Payroll batch processing
- IESS calculations
- Décimos management
- Liquidaciones

### 3.3 Warehouse Manager
- Guía de Remisión generation
- Driver/vehicle management
- Stock movements

### 3.4 Cashier (POS)
- POS invoice generation
- Customer identification ($50 rule)
- Receipt printing
